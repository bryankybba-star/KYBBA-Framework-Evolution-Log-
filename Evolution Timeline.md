KYBBA Framework Evolution Timeline

Purpose

The Evolution Timeline provides a chronological record of the KYBBA Framework's development. It documents the progression of Evolution Cycles, major milestones, and official Framework Versions.

Unlike the Framework Evolution Log (FEL), which records individual architectural decisions, the Evolution Timeline records the overall progress of the framework from its inception to future releases.

---

Framework Development Journey

Framework Initiation

Status: Completed

The KYBBA Framework project was initiated with the objective of developing a structured architectural framework for understanding, designing, and evaluating complex locations through systematic architectural reasoning and design principles.

Timeline Maintenance Policy

The Evolution Timeline is updated only when one of the following occurs:

- An Evolution Cycle is completed.
- A new Evolution Cycle begins.
- A Framework Version is officially released.
- A significant framework milestone is achieved.

Minor refinements, discussions, and individual Framework Evolution Log (FEL) entries do not modify this timeline unless they represent a major progression in the framework's development.
