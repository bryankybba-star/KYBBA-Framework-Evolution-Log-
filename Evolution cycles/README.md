---

Purpose

The Evolution Cycles directory contains the complete chronological engineering history of the KYBBA Framework.

Each Evolution Cycle represents a completed phase of framework development focused on a specific architectural or design objective.

An Evolution Cycle preserves the discoveries, decisions, principles, and engineering records produced during that stage before the framework progresses to the next phase.

---

Structure

Each Evolution Cycle follows a standardized architecture to ensure consistency throughout the framework's development.

A completed Evolution Cycle contains:

- README.md
- Summary.md
- Framework Evolution Log (FEL) entries
- Architectural Insights
- Emergent Patterns
- Design Principles
- Candidate Principles
- References

Development Philosophy

Evolution Cycles are sequential.

Each completed cycle establishes the architectural foundation upon which the next cycle is built.

A completed Evolution Cycle becomes part of the permanent engineering history of the KYBBA Framework and is preserved as a historical record.

Future discoveries do not overwrite previous Evolution Cycles. Instead, they are documented within new Evolution Cycles, allowing the complete architectural evolution of the framework to remain transparent and traceable.
