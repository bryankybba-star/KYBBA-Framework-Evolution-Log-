FEL-006

Title

Adoption of Discovery-Driven Framework Development Methodology

---

Evolution Cycle

Evolution Cycle 1

---

Stage

Structural Roles

---

Background

As the framework evolved, two approaches to development became apparent.

The first involved defining the architecture in advance based on assumptions.

The second involved allowing the architecture to emerge through structured evaluation, refinement, and evidence gathered during development.

---

Decision

The KYBBA Framework adopts a discovery-driven development methodology.

Architectural decisions will emerge through systematic reasoning, comparison, refinement, and validation rather than predetermined assumptions.

---

Reasoning

This methodology allows the framework to remain adaptable while preserving architectural consistency.

Each major discovery becomes part of the documented engineering history, ensuring that future refinements are grounded in evidence rather than opinion.

---

Framework Impact

The Framework Evolution Log became the permanent mechanism for documenting this process.

Future Evolution Cycles will continue to refine the framework through evidence-based architectural development while maintaining a complete historical record of every significant decision.

---

Validation Status

Ongoing Throughout Future Evolution Cycles
