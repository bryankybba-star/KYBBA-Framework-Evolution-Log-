FEL-003

Title

Discovery of System Capacities

---

Evolution Cycle

Evolution Cycle 1

---

Stage

Structural Roles

---

Background

Structural Roles initially described only the contribution made by each Entity Type Category within a Location.

As development progressed, a deeper architectural relationship emerged.

---

Discovery

Every Structural Role represented a unique System Capacity that enabled the framework to perform higher-level functions.

---

System Capacities Identified

- Population → Human Participation Capacity
- Organizations → Structured Coordination Capacity
- Capital → Financial Mobilization Capacity
- Resources → Material Provision Capacity
- Infrastructure Assets → Physical Support Capacity
- Information → Knowledge Continuity Capacity

---

Framework Impact

System Capacities became the architectural bridge between framework Design and Architecture.

This discovery established a consistent explanation for how architectural behavior emerges from Entity Type Categories.
