FEL-001

Title

Framework Development Sequence Established

---

Evolution Cycle

Evolution Cycle 1

---

Stage

Structural Roles

---

Background

Following the completion of the Entity Type Categories stage, the next phase of framework development had not yet been determined. Multiple development paths were available, each influencing the future architecture of the framework.

---

Problem

The framework required a development sequence that would minimize redesign while providing a stable architectural foundation for future stages.

The alternatives considered were:

- Defining Individual Entity Types immediately.
- Developing Entity Dependency Architecture.
- Establishing Structural Roles.

---

Decision

Structural Roles were selected as the immediate stage following Entity Type Categories.

---

Reasoning

Every future Entity Type should inherit an already established Structural Role before further decomposition.

Defining Structural Roles first provides a stable architectural foundation that supports future stages without requiring repeated redesign.

---

Framework Impact

The official framework development sequence became:

WORLD

↓

LOCATION

↓

ENTITY TYPE CATEGORIES

↓

STRUCTURAL ROLES

Future architectural stages will build upon this sequence.
